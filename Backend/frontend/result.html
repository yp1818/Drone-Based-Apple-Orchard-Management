Treatment Recommendations

  • Prune heavily infected branches
  • Remove fallen leaves and debris

Short-term Plan

  • Repeat fungicide application every 7-10 days
  • Monitor weather for favorable disease conditions
  • Improve tree nutrition with foliar feeding
  • Check for new infections daily

Long-term Management

  • Implement preventive spray schedule
  • Plant disease-resistant varieties
  • Maintain proper canopy management
  • Regular monitoring and assessment

Additional Notes

Apple Scab can cause significant crop loss if not managed properly. The fungus thrives in wet conditions with moderate temperatures (10-25°C). Focus on both chemical treatment and cultural practices. Consider rotating fungicide classes to prevent resistance development. Regular scouting and early detection are crucial for effective management.
